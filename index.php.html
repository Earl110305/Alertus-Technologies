About the System

The School-Based Earthquake Alarm System is designed to enhance classroom safety by providing immediate alerts when seismic vibrations are detected. Unlike large-scale alert networks, this system is placed in a single room and focuses on protecting the people inside that specific area.


Using a built-in vibration sensor and an automated siren, the system warns students and staff to take cover within seconds of an earthquake. It’s cost-effective, easy to install, and ideal for classrooms, laboratories, or offices within a school.

Key Features

Automatic Detection

Real-time vibration sensor detects seismic movement and activates the alarm automatically.

Room-Focused Alerts

Designed for single-room deployment to ensure localized and clear alert signals.

Audible & Visual Warning

Triggers sound indicators for quick response.

Backup Power

Operates during power interruptions using built-in battery support.

Easy Installation

Simple to mount and configure, with minimal maintenance required.
